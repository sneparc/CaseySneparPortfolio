About

My name is Casey Snepar. I have a Bachelors of Arts in the Classics from Union College in Schenectady, NY. After college, I went to Hunter College in New York and received a Masters of Arts in Adolescent Education Latin (Grades 7-12). Along with the Masters, I have Initial New York State Teaching Certification for Latin Education (Grades 7-12).  As a Latin teacher at the Williamsburg Charter High School, I have gained teaching experience in First and Second Year Latin classrooms. I also have substitute teaching experience in a Third Year Latin classroom. Other than that, I have substitute teaching experience in a variety of classes.

 In addition to teaching Latin, I have experience teaching Front End Website Development, including HTML, CSS, JavaScript, jQuery, SASS, the Command Line, git, and GitHub. I've held positions at The New York Code and Design Academy and The Williamsburg Charter High School.

As a Front End Developer on my spare time, I've built a variety of different static sites from my Web Development 100 and Front End Website Development classes at the New York Code and Design Academy.
